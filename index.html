WHO ARE WE

Established in 2019, Finance and Investment Cell, ARSD (FIC ARSD) is a student-driven body with a vision to create and cultivate financial awareness amongst the student community and to provide a common platform for all the finance and Investment enthusiasts to express, share, and enhance their practical knowledge and prowess in the fields of finance and investment. It aims to be a catalyst in developing practical financial knowledge and instincts amongst young minds through regular workshops, research sessions, webinars, and regular student discussions and making earnest efforts towards bridging the gap between classroom learning and practical application of financial knowledge to realize the variety of financial goals aspirations of the students

CONSULTING INITIATIVES

Our consulting wing works with local businesses and startups to provide financial analysis and strategic advice. We've completed projects in various sectors including:

  • CASE COMPETITIONS

    • FIC ARSD organizes various case-based competitions to challenge and improve the problem-solving abilities of its members and participants. The most recent event, CASE CLOSSEUM, was part of the flagship event Monetaire 2023. It attracted over 300 registrations from both national and international campuses and was judged by industry experts, providing participants with invaluable feedback and insights.
  • REGULAR SESSIONS

    • Regular sessions are conducted to enhance case-based awareness and consultancy skills among FIC ARSD members. These sessions focus on developing practical skills in case analytics and problemsolving, ensuring that members are well-equipped to tackle real-world consultancy challenges. Through these sessions, FIC ARSD aims to build a robust understanding of consultancy methodologies and best practices
  • GUEST SESSIONS
    • FIC ARSD regularly invites
    industry leaders and experts to the campus to share their knowledge and experience in the consultancy field. These guest sessions cover various aspects of consultancy, including client acquisition, client relations, and case management. By engaging with these experts, members gain a deeper understanding of the industry and learn about the latest trends and techniques in consultancy.

PROJECT PARCHAM

Parcham is the Social project Unit of FIC ARSD under which FIC undertakes initiatives to spread financial literacy and contribute its share to society. The Social Project department of FIC ARSD is its initiative corresponding to CSR, working with the intent of "Giving back to the society".

THE SOCIAL PROJECT WING


  • MONEY MANTRA SERIES :

    • With Money-Mantra, we took our mission online, breaking down finance into six episodes that made money matters easy, engaging, and accessible. From "Smart Saving" to "Investments Demystified" (stocks, mutual funds, and the works), we tackled topics that often seem intimidating. We even decoded credit scores and simplified tax basics as the new regime should not be a mystery to anyone. As the series unfolded, we proved that finance is not just for kids, it’s for everyone.
  • UDGAM DRIVE-I :

    • Our inaugural session with Warriors Without Cause NGO, on 23rd Feb 2025, brought together bright-eyed children aged 5-13 for their first proper introduction to money matters. Watching them role-play as shopkeepers and customers, carefully spending their pretend savings, we realized financial concepts aren't hard when taught through play. The way their faces lit up when understanding how ATMs work made all our preparation worthwhile.
  • UDGAM DRIVE-II :

    • Building on our momentum, we held our second workshop with Sankalp NGO on March 23, 2025, turning financial education into an interactive and engaging experience. Through games and real-life scenarios, we watched 10- to 17-year-olds pick up financial concepts that even adults often find challenging. Their curiosity kept us on our toes, sparking a lively discussion. Moments like these remind us why financial literacy matters
